Our Mission

Making Public Spaces Safer for Women

The Challenge

Over 80% of women worldwide have experienced harassment in public spaces. We're changing that through technology.

Our Solution

SafeWalk combines real-time safety features with community support to create safer environments for women everywhere.

Global Impact

Supporting UN SDG 5 by addressing gender-based violence and promoting women's safety in public spaces.

Supporting the United Nations

Sustainable Development Goal 5

Our project directly contributes to achieving Gender Equality by:

  • Reducing violence against women in public spaces (Target 5.2)
  • Enhancing women's safety through technology (Target 5.b)
  • Creating platforms for women's empowerment (Target 5.c)
